Summary

  • Auction-based buy live from Day 1 — Reach overshot universe (1.6M vs 151K) via broad geo-buying; ADT modest overall (+8 to +17pp)
  • Wk 1-3 Influencer window (HIGH CONFIDENCE) — ADT −4pp → +8pp; Disc Rec +28pp → +64pp; Disc Core +2pp → +23pp (peak). Highest uplift observed vs other periods, with low overlap with other interventions
  • Post Pricing film (Wk 4) — Overall ADT flat pre/post Wk 4 despite high reach in Week 4; Discounted Core largely flat across weeks; Discounted Recruiter flat vs Wk 3-4
† Attribution to a specific intervention is not clear — multiple levers overlap across weeks, so the observed lift cannot be isolated to one lever.
